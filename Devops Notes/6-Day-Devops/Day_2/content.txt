steps : 
1. open aws then go into ec2
2. go to instance 
3. click on the launch instance
4. Write the name of the instance
5. choose the type of instance
6. choose the operating system
7. choose the key pair
8. choose the security group
9. click on the launch instance
10. wait for the instance to be launched
11. go to the instance and click on the connect to instance
12. copy the public ip address and paste it in the putty
13. enter the username and password and click on the open
14. you are now connected to the instance
